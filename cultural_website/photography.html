Photography

The Photography event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 provides participants with an opportunity to showcase their photography skills. Participants are invited to submit their existing photoshoots based on the theme “Beauty of VTHT.” The event focuses on capturing the essence of the theme through a lens, highlighting the participant's ability to convey beauty through composition, lighting, and overall impact

Rules and Guidelines

  • Theme: Beauty of VTHT (Visual representation of beauty in the context of VTHT)

  • Submission: Participants must submit their existing photoshoots for the next round


Requirements

  • Photography Skills: Photos should reflect a high level of technical and artistic skills

  • Adherence to Theme: The photos should effectively capture the theme of "Beauty of VTHT"

  • Creativity: Participants should demonstrate creative thinking and unique perspectives in their shots


Note

  • Only existing photoshoots are eligible for submission; new photos cannot be submitted

  • Ensure that the photos are high quality and meet the event's requirements
