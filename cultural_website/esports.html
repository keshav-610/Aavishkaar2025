E-Sports

The E-Sports event is a competitive gaming showdown where teams of two face off in BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India). AAVISHKAAR-2025 offers a thrilling platform for gamers to showcase their strategy, teamwork, and gaming skills. Participants will compete in a series of intense matches with the chance to prove their dominance in the gaming world

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Maximum of 2 members per team (duo)

  • Game: BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India)

  • Round Structure: Best 2 out of 3 matches

  • Time Limit: Matches will be played until the winner is determined


Requirements

  • Equipment: Teams must bring their own gaming equipment, including mobile devices, chargers, earphones and accessories

  • Game Setup: Ensure the game is set up and ready to play before the match starts

  • Teamwork: Effective coordination and collaboration with your teammate will be essential


Note

  • One chance to compete for each team

  • Ensure that all gaming equipment is functional and ready for use before the start of the match


Registration Fee: Rs.400 per Participant
