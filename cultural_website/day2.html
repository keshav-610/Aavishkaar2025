Project Expo 2025

The Project Expo is a platform for showcasing innovative projects and ideas. This event invites participants to present working models, prototypes, or products in front of an esteemed panel of judges. It encourages creativity, problem-solving, and practical applications of technical knowledge. AAVISHKAAR-2025 offers an ideal stage for students to demonstrate their engineering skills and innovations

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Maximum of 4 members per team

  • Time Limit: Each team gets 10 minutes to present. Strict adherence to the time limit is required

  • Round Structure: Only one round with one presentation opportunity per team


Requirements

  • Teams must display a standup model, working model, or product

  • A PowerPoint Presentation (PPT) is optional

  • Power supply and electricity will be provided on-site

  • Teams must arrange the necessary components for their models or prototypes


Note

  • One chance to present for each team

  • Ensure the model is functional and displays technical prowess.


Registration Fee: Rs.500 per Team

Registration Deadline: March 1st, 2025
