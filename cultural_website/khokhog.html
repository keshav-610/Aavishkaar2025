Kho-Kho

The Kho Kho event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 provides an exhilarating platform for participants to showcase their speed, agility, and teamwork in this traditional yet thrilling sport. This event tests the players' stamina, reflexes, and strategic planning, offering a chance to excel in an intense, fast-paced competition

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Each team must consist of 12 players, with 9 active players on the field and up to 4 substitutes

  • Duration: The match will be played in 2 halves, each lasting 7-9 minutes

  • Round Structure: Knockout or league format based on the number of registered teams


Requirements

  • Gameplay: Players must demonstrate quick reflexes, agility, and precise coordination to tag out opponents or evade being tagged

  • Strategy: A clear and well-executed game plan, including chase rotations and evasion tactics, is essential for success

  • Team Dynamics: Effective communication and efficient player substitutions are crucial to maintaining energy and focus throughout the game


Note

  • Teams are responsible for bringing their own uniforms and appropriate sports gear

  • Matches will be officiated by experienced referees, and all decisions made during the game will be final

  • All participants must thoroughly understand and follow the rules of Kho Kho to ensure a fair and competitive environment


Registration Fee: Rs.800 per Team

Registration Deadline: March 1st, 2025
