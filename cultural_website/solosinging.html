Solo Singing

The Solo Singing event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 provides an opportunity for participants to showcase their vocal skills and musicality. Whether performing a cappella or with a backing track, this event allows singers to demonstrate their ability to captivate an audience with their voice

Rules and Guidelines

  • Time Limit: 2 minutes for the performance.

  • Music: Participants must perform a cappella or with a backing track (no live instruments allowed)

  • Theme: Open theme, but must be decent and respectful. The content must be suitable for all audiences


Requirements

  • Vocal Technique: Demonstrate vocal control, pitch accuracy, and breath support

  • Tone: The quality and richness of the voice, including clarity and consistency

  • Expression: Use of facial expressions and body language to convey emotions and connect with the audience


Note

  • Ensure that the performance adheres to the open theme and is respectful.

  • Participants should choose a song that allows them to demonstrate their vocal strengths


Registration Fee: Rs.300 per Participant

Registration Deadline: March 1st, 2025
