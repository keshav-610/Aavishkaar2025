Futsal

The Futsal event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 invites football enthusiasts to showcase their skills in this fast-paced and dynamic format of the game. This event emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and agility, providing participants with an exciting platform to compete and display their mastery of the sport in a competitive environment

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Each team can have a maximum of 5 players on the field and 3 substitutes

  • Duration: The match will consist of 2 halves, each lasting 10 minutes

  • Round Structure: Knockout or league format based on the number of registered teams


Requirements

  • Gameplay: Teams must demonstrate excellent ball control, quick decision-making, and effective communication during gameplay

  • Strategy: A clear game plan and coordinated efforts will be critical to success

  • Team Dynamics: Effective use of substitutions and player roles will be an advantage


Note

  • Teams are responsible for bringing their own gear, including jerseys and appropriate footwear

  • Matches will be officiated by experienced referees, and decisions made during gameplay will be final
