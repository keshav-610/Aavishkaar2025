Volleyball

The Volleyball event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 offers an electrifying platform for players to showcase their skills, teamwork, and strategic play in this high-energy sport. Participants will have the opportunity to demonstrate their expertise in one of the most popular and dynamic team sports

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Each team must consist of 6 players on the court and up to 6 substitutes

  • Duration: Matches will follow a best-of-5 set format, with each set lasting approximately 10-15 minutes

  • Round Structure: Knockout or league format based on the number of registered teams


Requirements

  • Gameplay: Teams must demonstrate precise serving, passing, setting, attacking, blocking, and defensive skills

  • Strategy: A well-coordinated game plan, including rotations and formations, is crucial to gaining an advantage

  • Team Dynamics: Effective communication, seamless transitions, and substitution management will contribute to team success


Note

  • Teams must bring their own gear, including jerseys and appropriate footwear

  • Matches will be officiated by certified referees, and all decisions made during the match will be final

  • All participants must adhere to the rules and regulations of the game to ensure a fair and competitive environment


Registration Fee: Rs.800 per Team
