ShortFilm

The Short Film event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 invites participants to showcase their filmmaking skills by creating a short film. Teams of two will submit a video that tells a compelling story, utilizing creative cinematography, editing, and narrative techniques. The event provides a platform for aspiring filmmakers to express their vision and creativity within a limited timeframe

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Maximum of 2 members per team

  • Time Limit: The short film must be between 5 and 10 minutes long

  • Format: Teams must submit their short films (videos) in advance to the AAVISHKAAR mail ID

  • Theme: Open theme, but must be decent and respectful. Strictly no vulgar or inappropriate language or content


Requirements

  • Storytelling: The film should have a coherent and engaging story that captures the audiences attention

  • Cinematography: The visual quality of the film should be clear and effective in conveying the story

  • Editing: Proper editing, transitions, and pacing should enhance the storytelling


Note

  • Short films must be submitted before the event to the provided email

  • Ensure that the content is respectful and aligns with the event's guidelines


Registration Fee: Rs.300 per Participant

Registration Deadline: March 1st, 2025
