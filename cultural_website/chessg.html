Chess

The Chess event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 invites strategic thinkers and tacticians to demonstrate their skills on the 64 squares. This event challenges participants to showcase their analytical thinking, planning, and decision-making abilities in a high-stakes, fast-paced environment following Blitz chess rules

Rules and Guidelines

  • Player Requirement: Each participant competes individually

  • Duration: Matches will adhere to Blitz chess timing, lasting 3–15 minutes based on the format

  • Format: Knockout or round-robin format, depending on the number of registered players


Requirements

  • Strategic Thinking: Players must display a deep understanding of chess strategies, including opening principles, mid-game tactics, and endgame techniques

  • Time Management: Efficient decision-making within the Blitz time constraint is essential

  • Focus and Adaptability: Remaining calm under pressure and adapting to the opponent’s moves will be critical


Note

  • Participants must bring their own chessboard and clock if required; the organizers will provide these if unavailable

  • Matches will be officiated by experienced arbiters, and their decisions will be final

  • Players should be familiar with Blitz chess rules and standard chess regulations for fair play


Registration Fee: Rs.300 per Participant

Registration Deadline: March 1st, 2025
