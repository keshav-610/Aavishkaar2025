Quiz Competetion

The Quiz Competition at AAVISHKAAR-2025 is a test of knowledge and quick thinking, where teams of two will compete in a fast-paced environment. This event offers a platform for participants to showcase their general knowledge, reasoning abilities, and quick reflexes across a range of topics. The competition will follow a format similar to popular quiz shows, making it an exciting challenge for all participants

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Maximum of 2 members per team

  • Time Limit: The duration of each segment will vary based on the format of the round

  • Format: The quiz competition will follow a format similar to the "Star Quiz" competition

  • Topics: The quiz will cover a variety of topics including general knowledge, history, science, sports, and more


Requirements

  • General Knowledge: Participants should be prepared to answer questions from a wide range of topics

  • Accuracy and Speed: The focus will be on providing correct answers as quickly as possible


Note

  • One chance to perform for each team.

  • Teams should stay alert and ready to answer promptly during the competition
