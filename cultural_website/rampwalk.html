Ramp Walk

The Ramp Walk event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 is a test of elegance and style where participants will showcase their confidence, poise, and overall stage presence while walking the ramp. The event allows individuals to express their personality through their attire and performance. Participants will walk to represent different generations, with the theme being disclosed at the time of the event

Rules and Guidelines

  • Time Limit: The ramp walk should last between 1 and 2 minutes

  • Theme: The theme will be disclosed before the event, based on different generations

  • Attire: Participants should wear formal or theme-based attire according to the given theme


Requirements

  • Confidence: Participants should display self-assurance while walking and posing

  • Poise: Smooth movements and posture should reflect grace and control

  • Stage Presence: Strong presence on stage with attention-grabbing performance


Note

  • The theme will be revealed just before the event begins

  • Ensure that your attire is appropriate for the given theme


Registration Fee: Rs.300 per Participant

Registration Deadline: March 1st, 2025
