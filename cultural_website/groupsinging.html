Group Singing (BAND)

The Group Singing (Band) event offers a stage for musical talent to shine through coordinated group performances. Teams can either perform original compositions or cover songs, creating a vibrant, musical atmosphere. AAVISHKAAR-2025 invites talented musicians to showcase their vocal harmony, instrumental skills, and energy in front of a distinguished panel of judges
