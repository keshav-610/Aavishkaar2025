Group Singing

The Group Singing event is a chance for participants to showcase their vocal skills as a team. Teams can perform any genre of music with a focus on harmony and synchronization. AAVISHKAAR-2025 invites teams to create a beautiful blend of voices, accompanied by creativity and energy, that will captivate the audience.
