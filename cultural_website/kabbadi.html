Kabbadi

The Kabaddi event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 offers an electrifying opportunity for participants to display their strength, agility, and strategic thinking in this high-energy, traditional sport. This event emphasizes teamwork, endurance, and tactical skills, challenging players to excel in both attack and defense

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Each team must consist of 7 players on the field and up to 5 substitutes

  • Duration: The match will be played in 2 halves, each lasting 10-15 minutes

  • Round Structure: Knockout or league format depending on the number of registered teams


Requirements

  • Gameplay: Players must exhibit precision in strokes, effective ball placement, and consistency in rallies

  • Strategy: Tactical serves, returns, and game planning are essential for securing an advantage

  • Focus and Endurance: Maintaining concentration and physical stamina throughout the match will be critical


Note

  • Participants must bring their own paddle; balls and tables will be provided by the organizers

  • Matches will be officiated by certified referees, and all decisions made during gameplay will be final

  • Ensure familiarity with the standard rules of table tennis for a fair and competitive match


Registration Fee: Rs.800 per Team
