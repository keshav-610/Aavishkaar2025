Table Tennis

The Table Tennis event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 offers participants the chance to showcase their precision, agility, and tactical gameplay in this fast-paced sport. Players will compete head-to-head, demonstrating their mastery of the paddle and ball in a highly competitive environment

Rules and Guidelines

  • Player Requirement: Each participant competes individually

  • Duration: Matches will be played in a best 3 out of 5 games format, with each game lasting approximately 10 minutes

  • Format: Knockout or league format, depending on the number of registered participants


Requirements

  • Gameplay: Players must exhibit precision in strokes, effective ball placement, and consistency in rallies

  • Strategy: Tactical serves, returns, and game planning are essential for securing an advantage

  • Focus and Endurance: Maintaining concentration and physical stamina throughout the match will be critical


Note

  • Participants must bring their own paddle; balls and tables will be provided by the organizers

  • Matches will be officiated by experienced umpires, and their decisions will be final

  • Ensure familiarity with the standard rules of table tennis for a fair and competitive match


Registration Fee: Rs.300 per Participant

Registration Deadline: March 1st, 2025
